Cascade Objectives

Step 1: Select the date from the drop down.
Step 2: The details will be displayed with edit, zoom and the add options. Click on the respective icons to do the actions.
Step 3: Click on “Add” Icon. The objective setting form will be opened.
Step 4: Captures the details of the objective driver, strategic objective, actions to achieve, tenure, budget and the expected outcome.
Step 5: Select the new or existing, objective type, role and the currency type.
Step 6: In the assigned role field, click on the right side symbol, the pop up will open.
Step 7: Select the options for single select or the multi select and the organization unit type. Click “Save”.
Step 8: Click on “Add KPI”. Captures the details of summary, type, target type, rating schema.
Step 9: After entering the above details, click “Add”.

Linked Objective

Step 1: Select the strategic objectives.
Step 2: The user can link the objectives here. That will be viewed for the user in the balance score board menu.
Step 3: Click “Add” to add the above details.
