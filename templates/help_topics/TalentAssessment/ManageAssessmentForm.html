Reviewer

Step 1: Navigate to the Talent Assessment>Manage Assessment form>Reviewer.
Step 2: Select the assessment type of 360 degree review or the manager review.
Step 3: In the access matrix, click on the field checkbox which are required.
Step 4: After selecting the details, click “Add”.
Step 5: To remove an incorrect details, click “cancel or clear”.
