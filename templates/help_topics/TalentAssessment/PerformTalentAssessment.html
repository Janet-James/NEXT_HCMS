Perform Talent Assessment

Step 1: Navigate to the Talent Assessment> Perform Talent assessment.
Step 2: In the Org unit field, click on right side button.
Step 3: The pop up will open and select the org unit type and the single type or the multi select. Click “Save”.
Step 4: In the employee field, click on right side button.
Step 5: The pop up will open and captures the first, last name and the identification number.
Step 6: Select the gender and then click “Search”.
Step 7: The values will be displayed and select them and click “Get check value”.
Step 8: In the role field, click on right side button.
Step 9: The pop up will open and captures the role name. Click “Search”.
Step 10: The values will be displayed and select them and click “Get check value”.
