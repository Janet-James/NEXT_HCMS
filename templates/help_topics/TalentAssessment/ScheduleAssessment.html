Schedule Assessment

Step 1: Navigate to the Talent Assessment> Schedule Assessment.
Step 2: Captures the details of schedule name and employees.
Step 3: Select the assessment category, employee type and the assessment type.
Step 4: After selecting the details, click “Add”.
Step 5: To remove an incorrect details, click “cancel or clear”.
Step 6: The created details of the schedule assessment will be displayed in the tabular column.
