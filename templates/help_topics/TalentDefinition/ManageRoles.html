Manage Roles

Every employee need to be clearly known about their designation. It includes the role details, Skill set, critical success factors, KPO and KPI’s.

Role Details

Step 1: Navigate to the Manage Roles> Role details.
Step 2: Captures the role title, work experience, role need, responsibilities, role need and the preferred education details.
Step 3: Select the organization unit type, role type, travel and the reports to details.
Step 4: After entering the above details, click”Continue”.


Skill Set

Step 1: Navigate to the Role definition> Skill set.
Step 2: The technical, functional and the behavioral competencies will be displayed.
Step 3: In each of the competencies, the competency summaries will be displayed in that user need to select the applicable field and then the required skill level.
Step 4: The required skill level includes the basic, intermediate and the advanced level.
Step 5: After entering the details, click”Continue”.
Step 6: The created details will be listed below.

Critical Success Factor

Step 1: Navigate to the “Critical Success Factor”.
Step 2: The user need to click “Add new” and enter the description there.
Step 3: After entering the details, click”Continue”.
