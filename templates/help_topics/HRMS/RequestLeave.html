Leave Request

Application Perspective from any users.
Step 1: Navigate to the Leave> Leave Request.
Step 2: The leave request can be raised with details of leave type, tenure, and reason.
Step 3: After entering the details click “Add”.
Step 4: If the permission is above 2 hours it would be noted as half a day leave.
Step 5: Email notification will be sent to both HR and leave requestor.
Step 6: The raised leave request details will be displayed in the tabular column.
