Application Perspective from Normal Employee Login
Step 1: Navigate to the Employees> Primary Information..
Step 2: The user has option to only view their information but cannot manage the data as the details are from the HR database. The Add/Update options as shown in the screenshots below will not be available for the normal employees.
Step 3: The user can view their own details and can also view the other employee details by clicking on the kanban view.

Application Perspective from HR Department User Login
Step 1: Navigate to the Employees> Primary Information.
Step 2: HR can add the new employee details, update and remove the details of an individual. Captures the employee demographic details.
Step 3: Select the company, department and the position of the employee appointed and also enter the matrix ID, date of joining and upload the image of an employee..
Step 4: Navigate to the Employees> Personal Information.
Step 5: HR can add the new employee personal details, update and remove the details of an individual.
Step 6: Navigate to the Employees> HR Setting.
Step 7: HR can manage the following information of the employees: the confirmation date, resignation date, total work experience.
Step 8: Navigate to the Employees> Skills.
Step 9: HR can add the new employee skill details, update and remove the following details of an individual: the skill name, experience and the rating.
Step 10: Navigate to the Employees> Education Details.
Step 11: HR can able to add the new employee skill details, update and remove the education details of the individual employee.
Step 12: Navigate to the Employees> Professional Experience.
Step 13: The HR can add, update or remove the professional details of an individual.
Step 14: Navigate to the Employees> Certification.
Step 15: The HR can add, update or delete the certificate details of an individual employee.
