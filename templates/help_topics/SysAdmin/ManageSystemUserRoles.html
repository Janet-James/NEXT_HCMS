Manage System User Roles

Step 1: Navigate to the Access control> Role Registration
Step 2: Captures the details of name, code and the description.
Step 3: After entering the above details, Click ”Add”.
Step 4: There is an option to update or remove the role details.
Step 5: The created roles will be displayed in the tabular column.
