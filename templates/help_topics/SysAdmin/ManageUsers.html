Manage Users

Step 1: Navigate to the User Management> User Registration.
Step 2: Captures the details of name, email, password, role and the group.
Step 3: After entering the above details, Click ”Add”.
Step 4: There is an option to update or remove the user details.
Step 5: The created details will be displayed in the tabular column.
