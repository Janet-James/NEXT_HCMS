System Analysis Step 1

Step 1: Navigate to the System analysis.
Step 2: Captures the details of the role, count, requested by, requested on, requirement type and the request reason.
Step 3: Click on “Go to mode selection”, it shows the mode selection screen.
Step 4: Click on the “View Consolidated Reports”. It shows the consolidated reports of all 3 analysis reports.

  1. Role: List of roles corresponding to the specific team / department.
  2. Count: Number of resources needed for the particular role under the department / team.
  3. Requested By: Shows who requested the need of the role.
  4. Requested On:Shows when the request was raised.
  5. Requirement Type:Shows whether it is internal requirement or external.
  6. Request Reason: Reason for requesting the number of resources of specific role under particular department / team.
