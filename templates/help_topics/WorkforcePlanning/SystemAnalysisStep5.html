System Analysis Step 5

Step 1: Navigate to the System analysis.
Step 2: Captures the details of the department, workforce summary, resource need, cost, total and the priority.
Step 3: Click on “Go to mode selection”, it shows the mode selection screen.
Step 4: Click on the “View Consolidated Reports”. It shows the consolidated reports of all 3 analysis reports.

  1. Workforce Summary: Summary for requesting the number of resources of specific role under particular department / team.
  2. Resource Need: Number of resources needed for the particular role under the department / team.
  3. Cost:Cost for the resources need.
  4. Total:Total number of resource need and the sum of cost.
  5. Priority:Priority for the resource need.
