Strategy Analysis Step 5

Step 1: Navigate to the Strategy analysis.
Step 2: Select the strategy and the STEEPLE impact will be displayed.
Step 3: Captures the details of role, WF need, WF available and the cost.
Step 4: After entering the details, click “Save”.
Step 5: Click on “Go to mode selection”, it shows the mode selection screen.
Step 6: Click on the “View Consolidated Reports”. It shows the consolidated reports of all 3 analysis reports.

  1. Role: List of roles corresponding to the selected strategy.
  2. WF Need: Workforce needed for the selected strategy defined in the step 2.
  3. WF Available: Number of existing resource under the particular role.
  4. Cost : Cost for the number of resources mentioned under the particular role.
