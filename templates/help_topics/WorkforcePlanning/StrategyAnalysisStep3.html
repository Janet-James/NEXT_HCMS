Strategy Analysis Step 3

Step 1: Navigate to the Strategy analysis.
Step 2: Select the strategy and then click on “show overall gap” button.
Step 3: Captures the details of task summary, workforce and the role.
Step 4: Captures the details of role, existing resource count, resource need, and the resource gap.
Step 5: After entering the details, click “Save”.
Step 6: Click on “Go to mode selection”, it shows the mode selection screen.
Step 7: Click on the “View Consolidated Reports”. It shows the consolidated reports of all 3 analysis reports.
