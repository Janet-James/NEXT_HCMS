Structure Analysis Step 3

Step 1: Navigate to the Structure analysis.
Step 2: Captures the details of the department, role, existing resource count, resource, gap and the total.
Step 3: Click on “Go to mode selection”, it shows the mode selection screen.
Step 4: Click on the “View Consolidated Reports”. It shows the consolidated reports of all 3 analysis reports.

  1. Role: List of roles corresponding to the selected strategy.
  2. Existing Resource Count: Number of existing resource under the particular role.
  3. Resource Need: Resource needed for the specific role under a department or team.
  4. Gap: Difference between the existing resource and needed resource under the particular role within a department or team.
  5. Total: Total number of existing resource count, resource need and gap.
